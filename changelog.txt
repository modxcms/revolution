xPDO 2.5.3-pl (Jaunary 3, 2017)
====================================
- Make sure xPDOQuery base class is always loaded

====================================

- Change constructor in PclZip to be PHP 7 compatible

xPDO 2.5.1-pl (November 14, 2016)
====================================

====================================
